**By John Christie, Co-founder and COO of Sinu**

Whether you know what business intelligence is or not, we all understand the importance of good data in our decision-making process. But where do we get the data to drive our decisions?And who’s got time to analyze it?

As your IT department, [Sinu](https://sinu2.squarespace.com/) manages a vast amount of data about your company. As your business partner, [we believe](http://sinu.squarespace.com/it-business-solutions/) the more we can present data about your technology in a human readable way, the easier it is to make good business decisions. With the right data at your fingertips, decisions can be made more easily and in a more informed way, and patterns and opportunities can be discovered and acted upon.
