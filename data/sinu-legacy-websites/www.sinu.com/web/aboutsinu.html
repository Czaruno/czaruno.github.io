Sinu delivers an all-inclusive, outsourced IT department for small business and non-profits called Sinu IT Service. Sinu IT Service is a superior alternative to traditional consultants and in-house IT, delivering unrivalled functionality, features and support at fixed cost with an average savings of over 60%. Sinu achieves these improvements by escaping the cycle of redundant custom solutions traditionally developed for small business. Sinu’s proprietary IT service application delivers, integrates and tracks all of a customer’s IT functionality, including communications, mobility, file share, security, backup and support.

Sinu IT Service is best suited for organizations of between 10 and 100 employees that value employee productivity. Sinu has customers across the New York metropolitan area, especially in lower Manhattan. Sinu management has over 20 years of experience creating technology solutions for businesses large and small, including Apple, Bear Sterns, Hypnotic and BBV Bank of Spain.
