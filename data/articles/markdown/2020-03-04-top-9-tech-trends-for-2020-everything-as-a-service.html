Everything-as-a-Service grew out of Software-as-a-Service (SaaS), which eventually expanded to a number of online services. With the cloud now supporting these and many other services, the term Everything as a Service – or XaaS – was developed to refer to the extensive variety of emerging services and applications that people can access on demand over the Internet as opposed to being housed on-premise.*
