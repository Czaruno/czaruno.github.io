Sinu is proud to announce that Electric and Sinu will merge in order to drive a more modern approach to IT support for businesses and nonprofits.

According to Information Age, “Electric says the acquisition of Sinu will add expert IT support to its deep bench of over sixty IT service professionals.”

“This deal is an investment in our customers. Sinu has proven that you can deliver top-shelf customer satisfaction to a wide range of businesses in a very repeatable, scalable way. I’m thrilled to partner and inject more of that DNA into all of our solutions here at Electric ,” said Ryan Denehy, CEO and founder of Electric, as reported by Information Age.

Larry Velez, CTO and founder of Sinu, added: “We founded Sinu to deliver overwhelming value to clients through a pioneering standardised, unlimited-support IT Department as a Service. With Electric, we have a partner who is accelerating service delivery through Artificial Intelligence. Together, our people and software will deliver unrivaled service. We are excited to shape an innovative future with the Electric team.”
