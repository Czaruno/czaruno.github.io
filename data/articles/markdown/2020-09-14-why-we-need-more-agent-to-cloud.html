In ancient times, business solutions like antivirus were managed by a server in the office in a client/server model. In today’s pandemic-affected world, there may be nowhere to put that server or many people are at home away from that server. This means that these needs must be solved another way – agent to cloud is the model for these needs in a 2020 world. Read more from Sinu CTO, Larry Velez.
