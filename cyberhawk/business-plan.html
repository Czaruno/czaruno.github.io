01 / Executive Summary

The Structural Shift

CyberHawk Cab Company will operate a localized autonomous fleet of 30 Tesla Cybercabs. When Elon Musk positioned the Cybercab at a sub-$30,000 entry point, he unlocked a new asset class.

Historically, deploying capital in transportation yielded depreciating hardware while requiring expensive human labor. Now, an individual layer can buy mechanical autonomy and generate passive network income.

The question is no longer "What does the car cost?" But rather: "How much does the node earn?"
