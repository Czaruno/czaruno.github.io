About Larry Velez

Short Bio — 25 Words

Larry Velez is a technology entrepreneur building Kogi AI, which uses advanced AI to transform mechanical documentation. Previously founded Sinu, acquired by Electric.ai.

Standard Bio — 100 Words

Larry Velez is a technology entrepreneur and founder of Kogi AI, a company using advanced AI to generate interactive 3D documentation for mechanical machines. With over 20 years of experience in technology startups, Larry previously founded Sinu, one of the first IT Managed Service Providers with a SaaS model, which was acquired by Electric.ai. He has been part of multiple zero-to-one venture-funded startups across payments, streaming video, and enterprise software. Larry is also a motoring enthusiast with a passion for precision engineering and functional design.

Extended Bio — 250 Words

Larry Velez is a technology entrepreneur focused on transforming how mechanical documentation is created and consumed through AI. As founder and CEO of Kogi AI, he's building interactive 3D documentation for machines ranging from cars to rockets, making repair and maintenance accessible on any device from smartphones to AR headsets. Larry's career spans over two decades of building and scaling technology companies. He founded Sinu, one of the first IT Managed Service Providers with a SaaS model, which was acquired by Electric.ai, the leader in AI Helpdesk Agentic solutions. He has been part of multiple zero-to-one venture-funded startups across sectors including payments, streaming video, and enterprise software. His writing on technology, AI ethics, and indigenous peoples' recognition has appeared in various publications, often addressing emerging trends years before mainstream adoption. Larry is deeply influenced by precision engineering and design philosophy. Beyond technology, Larry is an avid motoring enthusiast with interests in garage design, modern prefab architecture, and automotive restoration. He believes in functional excellence — when something has the same level of quality from every angle you experience it.
