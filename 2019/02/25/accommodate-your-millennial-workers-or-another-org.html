About the Episode

In this conversation with Dolph Goldenburg, host of the Successful Nonprofits Podcast, Larry discusses how organizations can better accommodate millennial workers—or risk losing them to competitors who will.

Key topics include mobile-first technology strategies, the shift to cloud computing, building trust in remote work environments, and an unexpected story about discovering a Banksy mural on the side of the Sinu office building.
